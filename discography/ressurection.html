TUPAC: RESURRECTION

Artist 2Pac
Release date 14 Nov 2003
Record label Amaru/Interscope
# discs / songs 1 / 15
Description Soundtrack for the 2003 Academy Award nominated documentary of the same name, containing some previously released songs and some new songs built around unreleased verses. It features 2 singles: ‘Runnin’ [Dying to Live]’, ‘One Day at a Time [Em’s Version]’. Certified 1x Platinum by the RIAA on 23 July 2014.
