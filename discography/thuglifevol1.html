THUG LIFE VOL 1

Artist Thug Life
Release date 06 Jun 1994
Record label Interscope
# discs / songs 1 / 10
Description The only studio album of Thug Life, the hip hop group formed by 2Pac, Big Syke, Mopreme, The Rated R and Macadoshis. It features 2 singles (‘Pour Out a Little Liquor’ and ‘Cradle to the Grave’) and was certified Gold by the RIAA on 24 January 1996.
