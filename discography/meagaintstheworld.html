ME AGAINST THE WORLD

Artist 2Pac
Release date 14 Mar 1995
Record label Interscope
# discs / songs 1 / 15
Description 2Pac’s third solo studio album, released while being incarcerated, featuring 3 singles: ‘Dear Mama’, ‘So Many Tears’, ‘Temptations’. Certified 2x Multi-Platinum by the RIAA on 6 December 1995 and awarded “best rap album” at the 1996 Soul Train Music Awards.
